Lip-Sync

Audio → video
Drop in a song, a voiceover, or just the hook, pick who performs it, and fal lip-syncs them to the audio. Two routes:
• Perform it — a character image mouths the track. Needs a mouth, so use Haze, a mouthed cast member, or a real face. Chieff is mouthless — for a Chieff music video, generate Seedance clips to the beat in the Video station and assemble them in The Cut.
• Sync to a clip — feed an existing video + the song and the mouth re-syncs (handles music).

Voiceover

Seed Audio 1.0
Character voices, narration and full audio scenes (dialogue + ambience + SFX in one pass) from ByteDance Seed Audio. Pick a preset voice or clone one from a clip, then write the line — describe who speaks, their emotion and accent for best results. Chieff is mouthless, so use this for narration over his scenes or for other characters. Output auto-saves to The Cut and can be fed into Lip-Sync. English & Chinese.
